About AFAC Rules

The AFAC (Algemene Fietsenstallingen Afspraken Code) rules establish standards for bicycle parking facilities in the Netherlands, ensuring safe, accessible, and well-maintained parking spaces for cyclists.

Safety First: All parking locations must provide secure and well-lit areas for bicycles.
Accessibility: Facilities must be easily accessible for all users, including those with disabilities.
Capacity Standards: Locations must meet minimum capacity requirements based on demand.
Maintenance: Regular upkeep and monitoring ensure facilities remain in good condition.
